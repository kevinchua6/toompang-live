FAQs

Q: Is the app available for download?

A: No. We are currently working on the beta version of the app for both the iOS and android platform. You can subscribe to our newsletter to get the latest updates.

Q: I own a car. How do I rent my car out?

A: Sign up on our app and when it’s ready, just submit your car's information, photos and driving license. We will do the rest of the work, including commercial licensing and insurance coverage for your vehicle .

Q: Will i receive compensation for interior defects caused by customers?

A: According to our terms of use, you will be compensated accordingly. We are still in the process of reviewing our terms of use.

Q: How will the cost of rentals be priced?

A: The variables that are included in our algorithms are duration of rentals, fuel consumed (chargeable after a certain milleage) and market supply and demand.

Q: I am an investor. How do I find out more information?

A: You can always reach out to us at support@toompang.com and our team will reach out to you as soon as possible.
